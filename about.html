About Us

Welcome to Camp Woofs and Wiggles, where every tail wags and every pup is family. We're passionate about providing exceptional care for your furry companions in a safe, fun, and loving environment.

Our Company

Camp Woofs and Wiggles is your dog's home away from home while you are at work or on vacation. Located in Lincoln, Nebraska, Camp Woofs and Wiggles offers a variety of indoor and outdoor spaces for your dog to run, play, and adventure with their new-found friends.

Our trained staff will ensure that your furry family member remains under a watchful eye and will have all of their needs met whether they are here for a day or a week. We understand that your pet is part of your family, and we treat every dog with the same love and attention we would give our own.

Since opening our doors, we've built a reputation for excellence in pet care. Our state-of-the-art facility features climate-controlled play areas, secure outdoor spaces, and comfortable accommodations designed specifically with your dog's comfort and safety in mind.

Our Mission

At Camp Woofs and Wiggles, our mission is to provide your beloved canine companions with a safe, fun, and enriching environment, whether it's a day of play or an extended stay. Our dedicated team ensures your dog experiences love, adventure, and personalized care.

We believe every dog deserves to be treated as an individual with unique needs and personalities. That's why we take the time to get to know each pup and tailor our approach to ensure they're comfortable, happy, and thriving during their time with us.

Our commitment extends beyond basic care. We strive to create meaningful experiences that enhance your dog's physical health, mental stimulation, and social skills. Through structured play, positive reinforcement, and plenty of love, we help your furry friend become the best version of themselves.

Why Choose Camp Woofs and Wiggles?

Experienced Staff

Our team is trained in animal behavior, first aid, and CPR to ensure your pet's safety and wellbeing.

24/7 Monitoring

Live webcams allow you to check in on your pup anytime, giving you peace of mind while you're away.

Safe Environment

Our facility is fully secured and climate-controlled, with separate play areas for different sizes and temperaments.
