Our Services

At Camp Woofs and Wiggles, we offer a variety of services to keep your pup happy, healthy, and well-cared for. From daily play sessions to overnight stays and full grooming services, we've got everything your furry friend needs.

Day Camp

Give your dog a day full of fun, exercise, and socialization! Our Day Camp offers both indoor and outdoor play areas where dogs can run, explore, and make new friends under the supervision of our trained staff.

What's Included:

  • Indoor and outdoor adventure areas
  • Supervised group play with dogs of similar size and temperament
  • Nap time for full-day campers
  • Midday snack for full-day campers
  • Free medication administration
  • Individual attention from our trained camp counselors

Pricing

Full Day: $30 (1 dog)

Half Day: $20 (1 dog)

*Add $10 per additional dog, per full or half day

Overnight Camp

Whether you're away for business or vacation, your dog will be in great hands at our Overnight Camp. We provide comfortable private accommodations and all the same fun activities as Day Camp, plus extra care and attention during their stay.

What's Included:

  • All the services we provide to our full-day campers
  • Private "bunks" with cozy bedding for overnight campers
  • Individual webcams that allow parents to check in on their pups anytime
  • Evening and morning playtime sessions
  • Meals (or we can accommodate your dog's food from home)
  • A free bath and nail trim for guests staying 3 or more nights

Pricing

Nightly Rate: $40 (1 dog)

*Add $15 per additional dog, per night in a shared bunk (Max 3 dogs from same family)

Grooming Services

Keep your pup looking and feeling their best with our professional grooming services. Our experienced groomers provide gentle, stress-free grooming in a calm environment, ensuring your dog leaves looking fresh and feeling pampered.

Services Available:

  • Bath & Brush: Includes shampoo, conditioner, blow dry, brushing, and light trimming
  • Nail Trim & Filing: Careful nail trimming and smoothing to keep paws healthy
  • Ear Cleaning: Gentle cleaning to prevent infections
  • Full Grooming: Complete haircut styled to breed standard or your preference, plus bath, nails, and ears
  • Teeth Brushing: Add-on service for fresher breath and healthier teeth
  • De-shedding Treatment: Special treatment to reduce shedding and keep coats healthy

Pricing

Bath & Brush: Starting at $35 (small dogs) | $50 (medium) | $65 (large)

Nail Trim: $15

Ear Cleaning: $10

Full Grooming: Starting at $55 (small dogs) | $75 (medium) | $95 (large)

Teeth Brushing: $12

De-shedding Treatment: Add $20 to any bath service

*Pricing may vary based on coat condition, breed, and dog behavior

Important Information

All campers (Day Camp and Overnight) must be vaccinated, spayed/neutered, and pass our free temperament assessment. This assessment includes a complimentary half-day boarding session so we can get to know your pup and ensure they're comfortable in our environment.

Ready to book? Contact us to schedule your dog's temperament assessment or grooming appointment!
